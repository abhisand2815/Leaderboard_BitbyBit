The provided Google Sheet is private or requires authorization. Using offline Demo Mode.

How to configure Google Sheet for Real-Time Sync:
  1. Open your Google Sheet: Open Sheet
  2. Click the blue Share button in the top right corner.
  3. Under "General Access", click on the dropdown that says Restricted.
  4. Change it to "Anyone with the link can view" (or view/comment).
  5. Copy the URL from your browser's address bar and paste it in the **Leaderboard Settings**.
This does not make your sheet searchable on Google, it only allows people with the exact link to read its data.
